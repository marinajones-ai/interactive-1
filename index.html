TW2NTY8: A Digital Archive

Marina Jones | Interactive 1 | 2025-2026

My name is Marina Jones, and I am a full-time student at California College of the Arts in San Francisco, where I study Graphic Design as my major alongside Writing as my minor. I will be using this website to document my progress, experiments, and finished work throughout Interactive 1 this semester. This site also serves as a record of my last semester at CCA, a period that feels especially significant as I prepare to transition out of the school due to its upcoming shutdown. As a result, I am approaching this body of work not only as an academic requirement, but also as a personal archive of everything I have built, learned, and experienced here. Throughout this process, I hope to capture both my creative growth and the uncertainty of this moment.
